How to Use StageText

  1. Enter Your Script: Type or paste your text in portrait mode
  2. Customize Settings: Adjust scrolling speed, spacing, and theme to your preference
  3. Set Timer (Optional): Add a countdown delay before your presentation starts
  4. Start Presenting: Rotate to landscape or tap "Full Screen" to begin
  5. Control Your Text: Use pinch gestures to resize and two-finger drag to reposition

Tips for Best Results

  • Practice with different scrolling speeds to find your comfortable reading pace
  • Use the "Tight" spacing for continuous text flow or "Wide" for clear separation
  • Adjust text size based on your distance from the device
  • Choose a theme that provides the best contrast for your lighting conditions
  • Use the countdown timer to prepare yourself before recording
