Introduction

Welcome to StageText! This Privacy Policy explains our commitment to protecting your privacy and the privacy of children who use our app. StageText is a teleprompter application designed for users of all ages, including children 4 years and older.

We take privacy very seriously, especially when it comes to children. This policy describes our practices regarding information collection (which is none!) and explains your rights as a user or parent.

Information We Do NOT Collect

StageText is designed with privacy as a core principle. We want to be absolutely clear about what we DON'T do:

  • NO Personal Information: We don't collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or any contact information
  • NO Location Data: We don't track or access your location
  • NO Device Information: We don't collect device IDs, IP addresses, or any device identifiers
  • NO Usage Analytics: We don't track how you use the app
  • NO Advertising IDs: We don't use or collect any advertising identifiers
  • NO Photos or Media: We don't access your camera, photos, or media library
  • NO Microphone Access: We don't record or access audio
  • NO Contact Lists: We don't access your contacts
  • NO Account Creation: There are no user accounts, logins, or registrations
  • NO Third-Party Services: We don't use analytics, advertising, or any third-party data services
  • NO Network Communication: The app works completely offline and doesn't send data anywhere

🧸 Special Protection for Children

For Parents and Guardians:

StageText is safe for children aged 4 and up. We comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) by ensuring that:

  • We do NOT collect any information from children under 13 (or any users at all)
  • There are NO social features, chat, or communication with others
  • There are NO in-app purchases or advertisements
  • The app works entirely offline - no internet connection required or used
  • All content is created by the user and stays only on their device

How StageText Works

StageText is a simple, privacy-focused teleprompter app that:

  • Stores Text Locally: Any text you enter is saved only on your device
  • Works Offline: No internet connection is ever required or used
  • No Cloud Storage: Your content never leaves your device
  • No Backup: We don't backup or sync your data anywhere
  • User-Controlled: You have complete control over your content

Data Storage and Security

All text and settings in StageText are stored locally on your device using iOS's standard storage. This means:

  • Your data is protected by your device's security (passcode, Face ID, Touch ID)
  • Only you can access your StageText content
  • Deleting the app removes all associated data permanently
  • We have no servers, no cloud storage, and no way to access your data

Third-Party Services

StageText does NOT use any third-party services, including:

  • No analytics services (like Google Analytics or Firebase)
  • No advertising networks
  • No social media integrations
  • No crash reporting services
  • No payment processors

Your Rights and Choices

Since we don't collect any data, you don't need to worry about:

  • Requesting access to your data (we don't have any)
  • Correcting your data (we don't store any)
  • Deleting your data from our servers (we don't have servers)
  • Opting out of data collection (there's nothing to opt out of)

You maintain complete control: simply delete the app to remove all local data from your device.

Parental Rights (COPPA Compliance)

Under COPPA, parents have specific rights regarding their children's online privacy. With StageText:

Parents can rest assured that:

  • No information about your child is ever collected
  • Your child cannot share information with others through the app
  • There's no need for parental consent because no data is collected
  • Your child's privacy is fully protected

If you have any questions about your child's use of StageText, please contact us using the information below.

International Users

StageText can be used anywhere in the world. Since we don't collect or transmit any data, there are no international data transfer concerns. The app works the same way everywhere: completely privately on your device.

California Privacy Rights

For California residents: StageText fully complies with the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) and the California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA). Since we don't collect personal information, California residents don't need to exercise data rights because there's no data to manage.
