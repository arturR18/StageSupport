How to Use StageText

  1. Enter Your Text: In portrait mode, type or paste your script into the text editor
  2. Customize Settings: Adjust scrolling speed, spacing, theme, and timer as needed
  3. Start Presenting: Rotate to landscape or tap "Full Screen" to begin
  4. Control During Presentation: Use pinch gestures to resize text and two-finger drag to reposition
  5. Exit: Rotate back to portrait or use the close button to return to editing

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I change the text size during presentation?
Use a pinch gesture with two fingers to make the text larger or smaller while in landscape mode.
Can I adjust the scrolling speed?
Yes! In the settings, you can choose between Slow, Normal, and Fast scrolling speeds. You can also adjust the spacing between text repetitions.
How do I dismiss the keyboard?
Simply tap anywhere outside the text editor, or scroll down in the settings area to dismiss the keyboard.
What's the difference between Tight and Wide spacing?
Tight spacing creates minimal gap between scrolling text repetitions for continuous flow. Wide spacing adds more gap for clearer separation.
Can I use StageText offline?
Yes! StageText works completely offline. No internet connection is required.
